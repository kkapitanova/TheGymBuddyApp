TheGymBuddy

User Guide

The Gym Buddy provides a quick and easy solution to tracking your workouts. It allows fast recording of workouts so you can get back to your workout or have the workout ready beforehand.

Navigation Bar

At the bottom of the screen you can always see a navigation bar that can lead you to 4 different screens. One of them is this exact user guide which aims to explain how to use this application and what features it provides.

Workouts Archive

Clicking the dumbbell icon will lead you to an archive of all the workouts you have recorded inside this app.

Adding New Workouts

Clicking the plus icon will lead you to a screen where you can add new workouts to the database. Every new workout will appear inside the workouts archive and you will receive a notification to confirm it has been added successfully. Your profile will be updated to show the correct amount of total workouts done.

Profile

Clicking the person icon will take you to your own profile, where you can see your profile, how many workouts you've done and can log out out from there.

Disclaimer

This app aims to serve as a diary where you can log your workouts and does not provide any advice on what exercises to do and how often to workout, please consult with a professional regarding this. You should not engage in workouts if your health does not allow to do so and should rest when necessary.
