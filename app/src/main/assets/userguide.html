TheGymBuddy

User Guide

The Gym Buddy provides a quick and easy solution to tracking your workouts. It allows fast recording of workouts so you can get back to your workout or have the workout ready beforehand.

Navigation Bar

At the bottom of the screen you can always see a navigation bar that can lead you to 4 different screens. One of them is this exact user guide which aims to explain how to use this application and what features it provides.

Workouts Archive

Clicking the dumbbell icon will lead you to an archive of all the workouts you have done and recorded inside this app. You will be able to search through them to find the workout you are looking for.

Adding New Workouts

Clicking the plus icon will lead you to a screen where you can add new workouts to the database. Every new workout will appear inside the workouts archive.

Profile

Clicking person icon will take you to your own profile, where you can see statistics based on your activity level. You can configure your account from there.
